# 1: Statistics menu: choose the following
# 	2: Pop variables into your country, to save and analyze separately in excel
#	3: How has your country evolved: population (u/c/t + var) by region, number of provinces (and change) also present
#		301: How has your country evolved. Including your vassals (western mediterranean, for Rome games)
#		302: How has your country evolved. Including your vassals (east med / persia, for Persia/Babylon games)
#		303: How has your country evolved. Including your vassals (far east, for China games)

#		402: Main variations, together (as in the event 3, but for the selected area)
#		403: the effect of the above
#		404: Rural: abs + growth
#		405: Urban: abs + growth
#		406: Upper: abs + growth
#		407: Potential: % left to grow (-20%, -10, 0, 10, 20, 30, up to 80%+)
#		408: Effective information range (migration range)
#		409: How many X things has there been, positive effects: grown + promotions + immigration
#		410: How many X things has there been, negative effects: famine + demotions + emigration
#		411: How many X things has there been, war-related effects: enslaved + killed
#
#	5: Popstatistics Dev Tools(tm), very powerful events that will affect all the world's population depending on current capacities. 
#
#	In the map modifiers, everything will have a color, from a 12-color gradient
